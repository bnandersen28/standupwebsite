About Stand Up!

Stand up! For Yourself and Others is an anti-bullying initiative sponsored by HART (Historic Area Religions Together, an organization representing various faith communities in the Williamsburg Area. The initiative was founded in ________, in response to the growing concern of the effects of bullying on our children. the importance of valuing individuality.
