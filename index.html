Helping Stop Bullying

Stand Up for Yourself and Others initiative, proudly sponsored by HART is on a mission to reduce the negative effects of anti-group bias on children in our community . Through engaging art and literature programs, such as story hours and puppet shows, we partner with local libraries and faith communities to teach the importance of individual differences. Join us in fostering a more inclusive environment for all!

Get Involved

We organize various events and workshops to educate children and parents about bullying. Our upcoming event is a story hour at the Williamsburg Regional Library. Join us and be part of the movement to stop bullying and create a more inclusive community.
